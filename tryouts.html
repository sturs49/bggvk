Interested in becoming a Bethpage Golden Girl? Tryouts for the 2026–2027 season are open to current 8th–11th graders. Start with our prep clinics — you'll learn the tryout routine and practice the required skills before the big day.

Fill out the interest form in our bio to let us know you're coming. Tryout information and forms will be handed out at the first clinic.

👟

Bring sneakers & jazz shoes to all clinics and tryouts.

📋

FamilyID must be up to date — you will not be permitted to try out without it. Start updating it now.

📄

Tryout forms will be distributed at the first clinic on May 6th.

📅

All three tryout dates are required. If you cannot attend one, explain in the interest form.
